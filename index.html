Group Operations
Career Model

Group Operations is the engine room of the Commonwealth Bank Group, dedicated to operating and enhancing processes to create experiences our customers will love. We proudly service multiple brands within the Group and deliver a highly flexible operations environment, outstanding customer experiences and better risk outcomes. We have invested in a range of exciting new business capabilities such as Digital Operations, Robotics, Process Architecture, Analytics, Productivity and Risk Credit, providing a diverse portfolio of role opportunities.

What is the Group Operations Career Model?

The GO Career Model is a framework to help support your career progression. It provides an overarching view of the many role types and opportunities that exist within GO. The Career Model helps you easily understand how your current role and skills align to other opportunities within the Group Operations framework so you can expand your abilities, seek new career experiences and meet your professional goals.
